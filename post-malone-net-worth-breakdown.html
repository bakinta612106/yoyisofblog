Early Life of Post Malone

Born on July 4, 1995, in Syracuse, New York, as Austin Richard Post, Post Malone emerged as a pivotal figure in contemporary music.

His upbringing was nomadic, owing to his military family background, which instilled in him a love for music from an early age.

Proficient in guitar, piano, and drums, he embraced a wide range of musical genres, encompassing hip hop, country, rock, and metal.

Embarking on his musical journey in 2011, Post Malone gained prominence with his debut single “White Iverson” in 2015, which reached number 14 on the US Billboard Hot 100 chart.

Inking a contract with Republic Records, he released his first album, “Stoney,” in 2016.

The album included the diamond-certified track “Congratulations” featuring Quavo, and it maintained a record 77 weeks on the US Billboard Top R&B/Hip-Hop Albums chart.

His second album, “Beerbongs & Bentleys” (2018), debuted at the pinnacle of the US Billboard 200 and shattered streaming records.

Notable hits such as “Rockstar” featuring 21 Savage and “Psycho” featuring Ty Dolla Sign emerged from this album.

The album was a nominee for the 61st Grammy Awards’ Album of the Year. His third album, “Hollywood’s Bleeding” (2019), featuring the chart-topping single “Sunflower” with Swae Lee, continued his trajectory of success, with “Sunflower” becoming the highest-certified RIAA song in history at 17x platinum.

Further achievements followed with “Circles,” his fourth US number-one song, and the most-played radio track of 2020.

His fourth album, “Twelve Carat Toothache,” debuted in June 2022, led by top-ten hits “I Like You (A Happier Song)” featuring Doja Cat and “One Right Now” with the Weeknd.

Personal Life

Currently unattached, Post Malone has navigated various relationships.

He was in a romantic partnership with Ashlen Diaz, a music promoter and booking agent, from 2015 to 2018.

They share a son, Austin Richard Post Jr., born on June 4, 2017.

While their romantic involvement concluded in 2018, they remain amicable co-parents.

Another chapter of his love life encompassed a relationship with MeLoveMeAlot, a Korean rapper, model, and artist, lasting from 2020 to early 2021.

Their connection originated on Instagram, and despite the long-distance nature of their relationship, they parted ways due to divergent schedules and lifestyles.
