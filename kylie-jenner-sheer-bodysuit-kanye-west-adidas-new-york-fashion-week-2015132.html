Kylie Jenner Models Sheer Bodysuit for Kanye West at Fashion Week: Pic

 Posted on August 19, 2024  |  2 minutes  |  262 words  |  Kelle Repass

Kendall’s not the only Jenner with modeling skills!

Kanye West hosted a fashion show in NYC on Thursday, Feb. 12, to launch his collaboration with Adidas. But instead of turning to his model-of-the-moment sister-in-law Kendall Jenner to work his designs, he asked her sister Kylie to hit the runway instead!

The Instagram queen, 17, donned a sheer bodysuit, olive sports bra, mocha booty shorts, and chocolate over-the-knee boots at the event, hosted at Skylight Clarkson Square. The sporty look was made complete with the addition of an ebony shearling coat.

As for beauty, Jenner covered her long tresses with a sheer cap. The PacSun designer also ditched her signature, plump plum pout in favor of a nude hue, a complement to her minimally made-up face.

Of course, she wasn’t the only family member on the scene. Jenner’s half-sister and West’s wife, Kim Kardashian, watched her husband’s creations come to life from the comfort of her front-row seat, with her daughter, North West, 19 months, perched on her lap.

Nevertheless, Jenner was extremely grateful for the time to shine. “Kanye X Adidas. Thank u to Yé for believing in me,” she captioned a behind-the-scenes photo from backstage at the show, which she shared with her fans on Instagram.
