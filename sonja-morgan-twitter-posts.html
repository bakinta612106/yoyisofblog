Five of Our Favorite Recent Sonja Morgan Tweets

 Posted on August 14, 2024  |  3 minutes  |  545 words  |  Billy Koelling

Sonja Morgan doesn’t have a problem saying precisely what is on her mind, which might be why people can’t decide if they love her or hate her. Either way, she’s one real housewife with a real opinion and she’s not afraid to call out her New York City costars with her thoughts. For example, she thinks that everyone sucks up to Bethenny Frankel, and she’s really good friends with Luann even though they have their issues. Frankly, as a group of adult women with careers and families and their own lives to worry about, it’s difficult for me to process how they can stand around and argue and fight and scream obscenities with people on national television; but to each his own.

We’re all entertained by it, so we’ll leave the drama of life to reality stars with a glass of wine, our pajamas and the smug knowledge that the biggest drama in our own lives is being judged for actually getting dressed and wearing lipstick to drop our kids off at school (every day, baby). With that said, we do love Sonja Morgan. Her twitter is one of our favorites, so here you go.
