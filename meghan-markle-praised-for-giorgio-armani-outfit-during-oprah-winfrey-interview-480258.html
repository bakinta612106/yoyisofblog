Meghan Markle Praised For Giorgio Armani Outfit During Oprah Winfrey Interview

 Posted on August 29, 2024  |  2 minutes  |  395 words  |  Billy Koelling

Meghan Markle and Prince Harry sat with Oprah Winfrey for their groundbreaking interview Sunday night, and fans couldn’t stop praising Meghan’s gorgeous outfit. Meghan is pregnant with the couple’s second child, and though she hasn’t revealed her due date, reports say she is carrying a girl and is expected to deliver this summer. If Meghan was feeling stressed out during the interview because of her pregnancy, she certainly didn’t show it. The couple addressed many questions and Oprah Winfrey is being hailed for her excellent interviewing skills.

Meghan gave birth to Archie Harrison on May 6, 2019. With his second birthday right around the corner, all eyes are on Meghan as she is pregnant again. Sadly, Meghan and Prince Harry suffered a miscarriage in 2020 and they are beaming over their growing family.

Wearing a Giorgio Armani gown, Meghan was both comfortable and elegant. The gown is called the Embellished Half & Half Belted Gown, and it retails for approximately $4700. The gown belted at the top of Meghan’s abdomen and her baby bump was clearly visible. The silk gown features an abstract design on one side of the front panels, giving the dress a semi-metallic look.

Meghan paired the dress with Aquazzura Irresistible pumps. The footwear was the perfect accompaniment to Meghan’s Armani silk gown.

Meghan and Prince Harry gripped the nation as they opened up about their decision to step back from their royal duties only to be removed from the royal life entirely. Prince Harry revealed his father cut him off financially and even removed the security that he has relied on for protection since birth.

Response to the interview has been overwhelming with millions praising the couple for opening up about such a painful time in their lives and laying it all out for the world to see.
