Backpack Kid Bio & Family: Age, Parents, Sister, Height

Backpack Kid, age 19, was born as Russell Horning on 19 December 2001 in Lawrenceville, Georgia, the United States. He is among the two children of his mother, Anita Reed. Backpack Kid has a younger sister named Jill Horning.

Russell Horning to Backpack Kid; His Career Fame

Russell Horning was just a normal child until he began creating and posting his dancing moves on social platforms. His signature dance style named flossing got him enormous fame. In this dance move, Russell swiftly swings his arms while shaking his booty and maintaining a stone-faced glare, carrying his backpack.

In May 2017, Backpack Kid’s fame went to greater heights when he was invited by Katy Perry to perform on the Saturday Night Live show. He stole the limelight on the show showcasing his brilliant dance moves and impressing the audience. Eventually, he starred in Katy’s music video for the single Swish Swish.

Russell is popular on Instagram with over 1.7 million followers, where he regularly shares his dancing moves. Besides, he has a YouTube channel titled The Backpack Kid, where he has garnered around 468k subscribers.
