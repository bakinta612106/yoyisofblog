Welcome to Eden (Spanish: Bienvenidos an Edén) is a Netflix original Spanish thriller series created by Joaqun Górriz and Guillermo López Sánchez. It debuted on May 6, 2022. On May 25, 2022, Netflix announced that the series has been renewed for a second season, which would premiere on April 21, 2023.

The Spanish thriller-drama series plot centers around a group of young men and women who are invited to a paradisiacal island for an energy drink launch party. However, they quickly come to the conclusion that a cunning plan is at play and that they might have been imprisoned on a desolate island with a deadly cult. Following its premiere, the show received mostly positive feedback. The casting and mystery components received a lot of attention, but the overall plot was criticized for being too predictable.

In the show, Charly Escudero may appear to be the life of the party when he first comes to Eden, but he is still dealing with the murder of his younger sister, which he feels terrible over. While on the island, he befriends and eventually falls in love with Eden Foundation level 3 member Mayka. However, this romantic connection does not stop him from wishing to leave the island. Charly boards a boat to depart Eden at the end of the first season, but does he succeed? Let's find out.
