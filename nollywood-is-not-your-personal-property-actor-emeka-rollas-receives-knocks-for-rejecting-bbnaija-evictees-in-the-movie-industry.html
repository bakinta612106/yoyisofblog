Nollywood is not your personal property Actor Emeka Rollas receives knocks for rejecting BBNai

 Posted on September 3, 2024  |  2 minutes  |  326 words  |  Kelle Repass

Nigerians have slammed actor and President of the Actors Guild of Nigeria, Emeka Rollas, for rejecting BBNaija evictees in the movie industry, stating Nollywood should not be a dumping site for evictees.

This, however, came hours after the most talked about housemates, Boma and Tega who were caught under the sheet, were evicted last night on the BBNaija show. Although this is not the first time this would happen in an 18+rated show, the cause of controversy is that Tega, married with a child, committed an abominable act with Boma, stating she only acted based on the script.

Emeka, who felt displeased by this act, had taken to social media to reveal to his colleagues on the issue, as Tega would want to venture into acting in the Nigerian movie industry after her eviction.

He wrote:

Nollywood!

Not a dumping ground for evictees!!!!! Case closed

Agree with me on this

However, Nigerians have dragged him, stating Nollywood is not his personal property and should not decide who and who not to be in the movie industry, adding that some of the evictees are better than some Nigerian actors.
