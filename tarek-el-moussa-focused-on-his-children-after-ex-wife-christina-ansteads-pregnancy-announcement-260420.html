Tarek El Moussa Focused On His Children After Ex-Wife Christina Ansteads Pregnancy Announcement

 Posted on August 30, 2024  |  3 minutes  |  430 words  |  Tandra Barner

Tarek El Moussa is focused on his children, as his ex-wife, Christina Anstead announces her pregnancy with husband Ant Anstead.

The Flip or Flop star says there is nothing more important to him than being a father to his two children, daughter Taylor age 8, and son Brayden age 3½. He used social media to profess his love them as the news broke, they will have a new sibling next fall.

“My loves my life my everything this picture represents the most important part of my life…being a father!!!!!” the 35-year-old began a lengthy Instagram message that included a picture of himself and his two precious angels.

Moussa went on to explain that no matter what happens during his day, he never brings negativity home or around his kids. The reality TV star also shared that he can’t help but smile the second he gets around Taylor and Brayden.

The HGTV star is launching his first solo project later this year. He will mentor rookie house flippers, as they embark on the booming business. Although the new show is still untitled and does not have a premiere date, Moussa did share what fans can expect from the first episode. He works with a young family in South Central Los Angeles who has put in on the line to make a profit.

Tarek El Moussa is moving on from his divorce. He is focused on living life as a single dad, enjoying time with two children. As Christina Anstead prepares to have a baby with her new husband, Moussa is just happy to have his kids.

“When I have my babies, I come home and can’t help but smile. I smile because I know no matter what we all love each other, and nothing can break our bond. I just adore these two:).” He wrote in the post dedicated to his children.
