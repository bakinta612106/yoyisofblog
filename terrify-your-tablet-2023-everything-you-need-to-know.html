Terrify Your Tablet 2023: Everything You Need To Know About The Event

 Posted on August 24, 2024  |  3 minutes  |  529 words  |  Kelle Repass

Terrify Your Tablet 2023 is an annual event that offers multiple opportunities to get access to e-books on your device. The event is organized every year on October 31st, and every time the organizers choose different genres. This year, the theme of the event was horror, and it was a huge success.

The event was hosted by Horrorsmith, and many authors participated in it. Participating authors offered their e-books for free across a number of platforms, including Kindle, Nook, Apple Books, Kobo, and more. The event was a great opportunity for readers to discover new authors and explore different genres of horror and thriller books.

If you missed the event, don’t worry. You can still explore the complete information on the event and the participating authors by visiting the Terrify Your Tablet Facebook group. The group was created on September 27, 2023, and it is a great resource for authors to coordinate, ask questions, and collaborate. Anyone can find the group, but only members can see who’s in the group and what they post.

Event Overview

Terrify Your Tablet 2023 was a book event that took place on October 31, 2023, organized by Horrorsmith Publishing. The event was designed to provide readers with access to various horror genre books for free on multiple platforms, including Kindle, Nook, Apple Books, Kobo, and more.

Event Date

The event took place on October 31, 2023, which is Halloween day.

Event Location

The event was entirely online, and readers could access it from anywhere in the world.

Event Purpose

The purpose of Terrify Your Tablet 2023 was to promote the horror genre and provide readers with an opportunity to access and read horror books for free. The event was also aimed at helping horror genre authors to reach a wider audience and promote their work. Participating authors offered their e-books for free across multiple platforms, allowing readers to discover new authors and titles.

Overall, Terrify Your Tablet 2023 was a successful event that provided readers with a chance to explore the horror genre and discover new authors and titles.

Key Highlights

Featured Speakers
