Harrison Ford and Calista Flockhart keep it casual in stroll around Amherst after son's graduation

 Posted on August 17, 2024  |  4 minutes  |  721 words  |  Billy Koelling

Harrison Ford and wife Calista Flockhart kept it casual Monday as they stepped out in Amherst, Massachusetts, where they had spent the weekend celebrating their son Liam’s graduation.

Calista, 58, chose a classic white T-shirt and jeans for her stroll around the town that is one of the oldest in the United States.

The Supergirl actress wore natural looking makeup and covered her shoulder length hair with a white billed cap.

The Ally McBeal star wore comfortable white sneakers with black laces for her stroll. 

She wrapped a black sweater around her waist and was spotted with a black wrist guard on her right arm.

Harrison, 80, looked handsome in a blue button down shirt with rolled up sleeves, dark jeans and black sneakers.

The Shrinking star’s gray hair was closely cropped.

He wore dark aviator sunglasses.

On Saturday, the pair had no problem acting the proud parents at their son Liam’s university graduation Saturday.

The couple, who have been married for 13 years , tried to fit in with all the other parents who were watching their children receive diplomas from Amherst College.

Calista, looked stylish in a short sleeved white dress with a crew neck and lace panels across the bodice and skirt.

The Full Circle actress brushed her dark blonde hair straight and wore natural looking makeup.

She carried a cream leather handbag and a cream sweater.

The proud mom could be seen using her cell phone to photos of Liam, 22, as he walked across the stage.

Harrison, 80, looked dapper in a navy blue suit with a light blue button down shirt. His gray hair was closely cropped and he wore dark aviator glasses.

Both parents looked fondly at their son as they embraced him in congratulations.

Liam kept his look simple beneath his black cap and gown.

The graduate wore a pair of beige khakis, a white button down shirt and black sneakers.

The Emmy nominee announced she had adopted her son Liam as a single parent in January 2001.

‘I have always wanted to adopt a child and I am overjoyed that I have been blessed with a beautiful and healthy son,’ the new mom said in a statement at the time.

Speaking of her new bundle of joy, she admitted, ‘I’m completely enchanted and awe-struck.’

Proud: Ford and Flockhart had no problem acting the proud parents at their son Liam’s university graduation Saturday. Liam, 22, received his diploma from Amherst College 

One year later, she and Harrison met at the 59th Golden Globe Awards in 2002.

Calista was nominated for a best actress award for her role in Ally McBeal.

Harrison was there to receive the Cecil B DeMille Award from the Hollywood Foreign Press Association.

The couple kept their relationship quiet until they decided to go public, stepping out together at the Venice Film Festival in September of that year.

The pair lived together for nearly a decade before tying the knot in 2010 in New Mexico, where the action star was working on the film Cowboys and Aliens.

Liam was about nine when his parents married. Harrison was 67 at the time.

‘I’ve got five kids in total, so it’s not my first rodeo,’ the Star Wars star told The Independent .

Yeah, obviously, I’m getting older but I don’t relate to it very much. It doesn’t interest me as something to dwell on. I have a 9-year-old kid at home. I feel like the father of a 9-year-old.’

‘My older children have taught me a lot about parenting. My first child was born when I was 25. I reckon I’m a little better at it now.’

The actor has four older children from his prior marriages.
