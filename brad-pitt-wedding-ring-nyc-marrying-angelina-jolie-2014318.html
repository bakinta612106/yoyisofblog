Brad Pitt Flashes Wedding Ring in NYC After Marrying Angelina Jolie

 Posted on August 27, 2024  |  2 minutes  |  266 words  |  Tandra Barner

Married and proud! Now that the cat's out of the bag about Brad Pitt and Angelina Jolie's secret wedding last Saturday, Aug. 23, the couple is happily flashing their wedding rings while out and about. 

The Fury star, 50, was spotted walking the streets of New York City on Saturday, Aug. 30, wearing his Robert Procop-designed wedding band while carrying a bright orange folder and wearing a matching fedora. See more pictures of Brad's ring.

The Oscar winner was in the city to film a star-studded commercial for tycoon James Packer, who owns several Asian casinos. The clip is being directed by Martin Scorsese and also stars Leonardo DiCaprio and Robert De Niro. 

Pitt's new bride, 39, was also photographed wearing her ring while touring the island of Malta, where the couple is set to film their new movie By the Sea, which will be written and directed by Jolie.

As previously reported, the A-listers tied the knot at a small wedding in France, and each of their six kids played a part in the ceremony. Maddox and Pax walked their mom down the aisle while Zahara and Vivienne threw flower petals, and Shiloh and Knox carried the couple's rings. 

"This was a total surprise, but something they were obviously planning on doing when the time was right," a source told Us Weekly of the exciting news. "Everyone is happy for them. It was small and family and friends only, just like they wanted. I'm happy they pulled it off. We all consider them married anyway, but now it's official."
