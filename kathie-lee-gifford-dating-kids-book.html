After her first marriage failed with a divorce, Kathie Lee Gifford married again. But this time, she had to lose her husband following some natural causes. Now after nearly five years of his departure, the media persona reveals that she is dating a mystery man.

Kathie Lee Gifford is a talented American television presenter who is best known for her 15-year run on the talk show, Live! With Regis and Kathie Lee. Besides her journalism works, she is a singer, songwriter, actress, and author.

Insights on Kathie Lee Gifford’s Bio & Family: Age, Parents, Education, Height

The 66-years-of-age versatile personality was born with the name Kathryn Lee Epstein on 16 August 1953, to parents, Aaron Epstein and Joan Epstein. She was born in Paris, France as her father, Aaron, a musician and former US Navy Chief Petty Officer, was stationed with his family in France at the time of her birth. Kathie’s mom, Joan, was a singer.

About 5 feet and 5 inches (1.65 meters) tall in height, Kathie grew up in Bowie, Maryland alongside her brother, David Paul Epstein and sister Michie Mader. There, she attended Bowie High School. After her high school graduation in 1971, Kathie enrolled at Oral Roberts University in Tulsa, Oklahoma, to continue her further education. At Oral Roberts, she studied drama and music.

Media Career: WABC-TV, NBC, Today

Kathie Lee Gifford registered a media breakthrough after replacing Ann Abernathy as co-host of The Morning Show on WABC-TV with Regis Philbin. It went into the national broadcast in 1988 as Live! With Regis and Kathie Lee.

Furthermore, Kathie joined NBC’s morning show, Today, on 31 March 2008, as co-host of the fourth hour titled Today with Kathie Lee and Hoda, alongside Hoda Kotb. Later, Kathie and Hoda replaced Ann Curry and Natalie Morales. It was on 11 December 2018 when Kathie announced that she would be leaving Today in April 2019.

Kathie left Today in order to pursue her movie career as an actress, director, and producer.

Her Entertainment Career, Net Worth

Kathie’s career took off as vocalist on the game show Name That Tune with Tom Kennedy in the 1970s. After that, she made guest appearances in numerous films and television series. It was after she left Today, Kathie decided to take this part of her career into new heights.

She filmed Then Came You in 2018 and also appeared in a Hallmark Christmas movie for Hallmark Movies & Mysteries called A Godwink Christmas. Kathie has several projects on her belt including sequels to Then Came You.

For her exceptional versatile career, Kathie Lee Gifford amasses a hefty net worth of $60 million. Besides, her annual salary figure touches the mark of $5 million.

Who Is Kathie Lee Dating Now?

Kathie Lee revealed in February 2020 that she is dating a sweet man five years after her husband’s tragic death. The 66-year-old media personality claimed that she was ready for love again but decided not to reveal the name of her new partner.

Speaking to DailyMailTV, Kathie quoted,

You don’t find love, love finds you. That’s what I’m waiting for. I’m not going out looking for it. God will bring it to me.

Kathie Lee Gifford pictured with her date (Picture: The Sun)

Although Kathie remained discrete about her new man’s identity, he was named by DailyMail as insurance agent Randy Cronk, a 56-year old Spring Hill, Tennessee resident.

Her Married Life, Kids

Kathie’s married life goes back to 1976 when she married Paul Johnson, a Christian composer, arranger, producer, and publisher. The married pair gained fame in Christian music, appearing on covers of several Christian magazines. However, their marriage was strenuous from the start and it eventually ended with a divorce in 1982.

After the first failed marriage, Kathie walked down the aisle with an American football player and television sports commentator, Frank Gifford, on 18 October 1986. Kathie welcomed a son named Cody Newton Gifford with her husband on 22 March 1990. Following that, the couple was blessed with daughter Cassidy Erin Gifford on 2 August 1993, who is an actress now.

Kathie Lee Gifford pictured with her late husband Frank Gifford (Picture: People)

Although the married pair was going well with their romantic life, Kathie had to let go of her husband following some natural causes. Frank died on 9 August 2015 at their Greenwich, Connecticut home, a week shy of his 85th birthday.

Some Interesting Facts About Kathie Lee Gifford

  • Kathie’s paternal grandfather was a Russian Jewish immigrant. Her other ancestry is English, German, and French.
  • In her media career, Kathie has received 11 Daytime Emmy nominations and won her first Daytime Emmy in 2010 as part of the Today team.
  • Besides a number of children’s books, Kathie has written two autobiographical books, Just When I Thought I’d Dropped My Last Egg: Life and Other Calamities in 2010 and The Rock, the Road and the Rabbi.
  • Kathie is a celebrity ambassador for the non-profit organization Childhelp.
  • Kathie named her dog Regis, after her famous former co-star, Regis Philbin.
