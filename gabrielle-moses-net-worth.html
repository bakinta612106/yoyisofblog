Gabrielle Moses Biography

Gabrielle Moses is best known as YouTuber. Beauty and makeup enthusiast who is known for her tutorials, cosmetic reviews, and various challenge videos on her self-titled YouTube channel, which has earned over 750,000 subscribers. She gained a bigger fanbase while collaborating with Jack Brinkman on the YouTube channel Jack & Gab up until the couple broke up in 2020.  She was born on April 6, 2000, in Las Vegas, NV. She played soccer and was a cheerleader in high school. One of her first appearances on the Jack & Gab YouTube channel was in a September 2016 video titled “GIRLFRIEND TAG (GONE WRONG!).”  She and Eva Gutowski are YouTubers whose content is dedicated to all things beauty related. She has a younger sister named Cassie and a twin sister named Madalyn. She was born in Las Vegas, Nevada, and relocated to Missouri at a young age. Her zodiac sign is Aries.
