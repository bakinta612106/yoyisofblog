Drake Jokingly Corrects Article Stating That He Helped Kehlani Name Her Album

 Posted on September 1, 2024  |  2 minutes  |  347 words  |  Tandra Barner

Not long before Kehlani’s record dropped, It Was Good Until It Wasn’t, it was reported by multiple outlets that she actually spoke to Drake before releasing it.

Reportedly, Kehlani chose to ask Drake for advice on what to name the record, considering the Toronto-native does have a knack for catchy titles, including If You’re Reading This…It’s Too Late, among others. Following the record’s release, Kehlani has been the subject of praise in her respective community.

Thus far, fans of the singer-songwriter have been stating that it’s easily one of her best projects. Her progression as an artist has been hailed by fans, critics, and her contemporaries as well. Additionally, the Toronto-native, Drake, shouted out to Kehlani.

He posted a headline on his Instagram Story which claimed he played a crucial role in the album’s title. The rapper said on the ‘Gram that the title of the article should be “Kehlani drops a life soundtrack,” rather than suggesting that he played a role in it. One of Drake’s producers, Boi1da, also helped Kehlani with her new project.

Drake has never stopped appearing in the headlines, however, in recent news, his child has been the subject a lot more. During an appearence on Young Money Radio, Drake explained what it was like to become a father for the first time in addition to new music.

As it was previously reported, Drake tried to keep his personal life under wraps as people began speculating that he had a baby. His child, Adonis, however, was finally revealed by Drake and his baby momma, Sophie Brussaux.

Fans of the rapper will remember when Drake revealed a photo of his toddler on his IG back on the 30th of March. Drake stated it “was great” for him to do so. According to the More Life star, it felt as though he was sharing something he loved with the world.
