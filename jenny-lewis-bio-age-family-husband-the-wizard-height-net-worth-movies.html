Jenny Lewis Biography

Jenny Lewis is an American singer, songwriter, actress, and musician. She rose to fame as a child actress when she appeared in the film The Wizard, Troop Beverly Hills, and Brooklyn Bridge. Additionally, she was a lead singer, rhythm guitarist, and keyboardist for Rilo Kiley, the indie rock band.

Moreover, she was the starring in the film The Wizard which is an American family film directed by Todd Holland. It was written by David Chisholm and was Tobey Maguire’s film debut. In addition, she started the “The Voyager” tour at the Ottawa Bluesfest in Ontario, Canada.

Lewis appeared in commercials for the Black and Decker Popcorn Maker, Toys “R” Us toy stores, Mattel’s Barbie and Baby Skates dolls, and Kellogg’s Corn Pops, among others. Additionally, she was cast as one of Lucy’s grandchildren in the short-lived film Lucille Ball sitcom Life With Lucy.

Additionally, she has appeared in television shows for small roles such as Baywatch, Murder She Wrote, The New Twilight Zone, Growing Pains, Just the Ten of Us, Roseanne, The Golden Girls, and Mr. Belvedere.

She also appeared on the game show Card Sharks on August 6, 1987, when she was 11 years old. Card Sharks was a kids’ week episode where Jenny procured $500 but did not win her match.
